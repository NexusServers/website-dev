How quickly can I get my game server online?

All Nexus game servers are instantly deployed after payment. You'll receive login credentials within 30 seconds, and most game servers can be fully configured and online within 2-5 minutes using our one-click installers.

Absolutely! You can easily upgrade your game server at any time through our client area. Our upgrade process preserves all your game data, configurations, and world files.

Many customers start with a smaller plan and upgrade as their community grows. We make this process seamless so your players experience minimal downtime during the transition.
